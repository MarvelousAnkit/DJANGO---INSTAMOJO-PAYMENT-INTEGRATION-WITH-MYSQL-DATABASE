Cancellation and Refund Policy

These are our cancellation and refund policies.

Refunds and Exchanges Terms and Conditions

There are NO REFUNDS on any ticket, gift voucher or product except as required by law (including the Australian Consumer Law);

The Patna Comedy Carnival will only offer a refund or exchange of a ticket if an event is cancelled or rescheduled (and you cannot or do not wish to attend the rescheduled event), or to the extent otherwise required by law
If an event is cancelled or rescheduled, all liability is limited to the amount of the ticketing transaction (including all booking fees). The Venue is not liable for any other losses incurred by you as a result of the cancellation or rescheduling of an event (including any travel and accommodation expenses).
All refund requests should be put in writing and directed to admin@akivahospitality.com and wherever possible please contact the Venue regarding this at least 48 hours prior to your event. Proof of purchase may be required for any refund or exchange.
Patna Comedy Carnival does not offer refunds or exchanges as a result of a change in your personal circumstances, change of mind or if you are required to self-isolate or quarantine (and the event falls within the isolation/quarantine period) due to:
a. being a confirmed case of COVID-19; or
b. having close contact with a confirmed case of COVID-19; or
c. living in or having been in a high-risk area within Australia; or
d. having returned from overseas;
In addition to this, Patna Comedy Carnival does not offer refunds of exchanges if you:
a. are unable to travel to attend the event due to border closures or Government lockdowns that were unforeseen at the time of sale;
b. are awaiting COVID-19 test results (and the event falls within the period of awaiting test results); or .
c. are currently feeling unwell with COVID-19 symptoms;
Any and all refunds will be automatically processed using the payment method employed by the patron in their original transaction.

Contact us at patnacomedycarnival@gmail.com for questions related to refunds and returns.
